Doctrine — carry these rules into ODH

Color restraint

Color appears only for (1) attention states and (2) the primary CTA. Everything else is neutral. Flat color is fine; flat surfaces are not.

Depth over flat

Every panel/card sits on a plane with a resting lift, tiering up on hover and selected. No dead-flat surfaces.

Pop via type, not color

Hierarchy is carried by scale, weight, and contrast. Big display type, oversized tabular numbers, tracked-caps micro-labels.

Signals never shimmer

The action gradient is for "go" surfaces only. Alert (red) and warn (amber) stay flat — a signal must read instantly, never decorative.

Numbers are the jewelry

The focal mass and its figures win the screen; the table recedes beneath it — flat, quiet, smaller.

Coding-model copy

The engine auto-codes all records; the human reviews. Copy says not yet reviewed / reviewed by you — never "coded". Re-word per program, keep the model.
